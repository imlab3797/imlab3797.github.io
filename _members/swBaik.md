description: >
  **Sung Wook Baik** is a Full Professor in the Department of Software and the Director of the **Intelligent Media Laboratory (IM Lab)** at **Sejong University**, Seoul, Korea. Since 2002, he has served Sejong University in multiple key leadership roles including Dean of two colleges, Managing Director of the National Program of Excellence in Software, Vice President of the Sejong Industry–Academic Cooperation Foundation, and currently as Vice President of Research Affairs. He has also served as General Chair of the International Conference on Next Generation Computing four times and collaborates with international research teams across Europe, the UK, USA, Brazil, Pakistan, Vietnam, and other countries.

  His research interests include image processing, pattern recognition, video analytics, big data analysis, multimedia data processing, energy load forecasting, IoT/IIoT, and smart cities. He has published over 200 peer-reviewed international journal papers in these areas, including venues such as IEEE TMC-Systems, IoTJ, COMMAG, FGCS, Pattern Recognition, Neurocomputing, PRL, IJIM, Springer MTAP, JOMS, RTIP, and others.

  For more details, please visit his **Research Profile** and **Google Scholar**.
