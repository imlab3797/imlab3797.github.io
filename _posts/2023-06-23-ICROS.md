We, **Seunghun Jang**, **Myeongseok Ryu**, **Park Jihyeok**, and **Kim Seonsu**, participated in the **2023 Institute of Control, Robotics and Systems (ICROS)** conference held at *Sol Beach* in **Samcheok, South Korea** from **June 21 to 23, 2023**.



Master student **Seunghun Jang** presented a paper titled <a href="{{site.url}}/publications/2023-Stator-Flux/">**Stator Flux Linkage Estimation Method of Synchronous Machines Based on Integration Error Estimation**</a> at the conference.
