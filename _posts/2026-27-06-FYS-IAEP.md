We are pleased to share that the <strong>FAIREEH International Academic Exchange Program – Global Study Abroad Pairing Day</strong> was successfully held on <strong>June 27, 2026</strong>, at the <strong>Daeyang AI Center, Sejong University, Seoul, Republic of Korea</strong>.

<p>
The program welcomed visiting high school students and teachers from Türkiye and featured a special lecture entitled <strong>"AI for the Next Generation"</strong>, followed by an interactive question-and-answer session and networking meeting. Participants explored the role of artificial intelligence in education, future career opportunities, and international academic collaboration. Certificates of Participation were presented to the students in recognition of their active engagement and commitment to global educational exchange. The event provided valuable exposure to artificial intelligence and higher education while strengthening international friendships and fostering future collaboration.
