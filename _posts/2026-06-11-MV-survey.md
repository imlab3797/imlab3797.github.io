We are pleased to announce our latest journal publication titled
<strong>"Multi camera connected vision system with multi view analytics: A comprehensive survey"</strong>,
published in the <strong>IEEE Internet of Things Journal</strong>.

<p>
This survey presents the first comprehensive review of <strong>Connected Vision Systems (CVS)</strong>, integrating multi-view multi-camera (MVMC) tracking, person re-identification (Re-ID), and action understanding (AU) into a unified framework. It introduces a novel taxonomy of CVS, systematically summarizes state-of-the-art datasets, methodologies, evaluation metrics, and benchmark results, and discusses emerging research directions including lifelong learning, privacy preservation, and federated learning. The survey aims to serve as a valuable resource for researchers and practitioners working toward the next generation of intelligent, scalable, and adaptive connected vision systems.
</p>
