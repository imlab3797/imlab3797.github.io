Master student **Myeongseok Ryu** and Undergraduate inters, **Jihyeok Park** and **Seonsu Kim**, received the **Best Paper Award** and **Undergraduate Student Paper Award**, respectively, at the **2023 Institute of Control, Robotics and Systems (ICROS)** conference held at *Sol Beach* in **Samcheok, South Korea** from **June 21 to 23, 2023**.


Congratulations to **Myeongseok Ryu**, **Jihyeok Park**, and **Seonsu Kim** for their outstanding achievements!
