2024.8.26., 최경환 교수님이 NUS의 ME Department Seminar에 초청되어 "Predictive Energy Management of Connected HEVs"를 주제로 발표했습니다.

Principal Investigator **Kyunghwan Choi** was invited to give a keynote speech titled <strong>Predictive Energy Management of Connected Electrified Vehicles</strong> at the **ME Department Seminar** hosted by the National University of Singapore (NUS) in **Singapore**, on **August 26, 2024**.
