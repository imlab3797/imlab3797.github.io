We are pleased to announce our latest journal publication titled
<strong>"Differential attention transformer-enhanced graph neural network for accurate material property prediction"</strong>,
published in <strong>Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence</strong>.

<p>
This work presents a novel <strong>Differential Attention Transformer-enhanced Graph Neural Network (DAT-GNN)</strong> for accurate material property prediction. The proposed framework integrates chemically informed structural features with self-attention, differential attention, residual graph convolutions, and positional encodings to effectively model complex atomic interactions in crystalline materials. Experimental results on benchmark Materials Project datasets demonstrate significant improvements in predicting formation energy and bandgap, highlighting the potential of the proposed approach for accelerating data-driven materials discovery and advancing materials informatics.
</p>
